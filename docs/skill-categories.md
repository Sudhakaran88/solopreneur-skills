# Skill Categories

The 42 skills in this repo, grouped by what they do. Each skill is self-contained — install the whole pack or cherry-pick.

## Foundation

Context-setting skills other skills read from. Install these first.

- `solopreneur-context` — Captures your business, ICP, offer, and constraints so other skills don't ask every time.
- `product-marketing-context` — Positioning, messaging pillars, and competitor context for marketing skills.

## Website & Audits

Full-site reviews and platform-specific audits.

- `website-audit` — Holistic review of a marketing site: messaging, structure, conversion, SEO, performance.
- `shopify-audit` — Shopify-specific store audit (theme, apps, product pages, checkout).
- `wordpress-performance` — WordPress-specific performance and Core Web Vitals review.
- `ai-readiness` — How well your site is set up to be cited by AI search engines.
- `saas-landing-page` — Teardown and rebuild framework for SaaS landing pages.

## CRO & Conversion

Per-surface conversion optimization. Pick the skill that matches the surface.

- `page-cro` — General marketing pages: homepage, landing, pricing, features.
- `signup-flow-cro` — Signup, registration, and trial activation flows.
- `onboarding-cro` — Post-signup activation and time-to-value.
- `form-cro` — Non-signup forms: lead capture, contact, demo, application.
- `popup-cro` — Popups, modals, overlays, exit intent, banners.
- `paywall-upgrade-cro` — In-app paywalls and upgrade screens for free-to-paid conversion.
- `ab-test-setup` — Designing and running experiments.

## Copywriting & Editing

Writing, rewriting, and polishing copy.

- `copywriting` — Writing marketing copy for pages, headlines, CTAs.
- `copy-editing` — Multi-pass editing framework for existing copy.
- `ad-creative` — Generating and iterating ad copy at scale for paid platforms.

## SEO

Traditional and AI-era search optimization.

- `seo-audit` — Technical and on-page SEO audits.
- `ai-seo` — Optimizing to be cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overviews.
- `aeo` — Answer Engine Optimization — structuring content for direct-answer engines.
- `programmatic-seo` — Template-driven pages at scale.
- `schema-markup` — Structured data and JSON-LD implementation.
- `site-architecture` — Information architecture, URL structure, internal linking.

## Email

Outbound, lifecycle, and ecommerce email.

- `email-sequence` — Drip campaigns, welcome, nurture, re-engagement, win-back.
- `cold-email` — B2B outbound: subject lines, opens, replies, follow-up cadence.
- `ecom-email-nurture` — Ecommerce-specific flows: abandoned cart, post-purchase, browse abandonment.

## Content & Social

What to publish, where, and why.

- `content-strategy` — Topic clusters, editorial planning, content-market fit.
- `social-content` — Platform-specific content for LinkedIn, X, Instagram, TikTok.
- `marketing-ideas` — 139 marketing tactics organized for solopreneur SaaS.

## Growth & Launch

Getting from zero to traction and beyond.

- `launch-strategy` — Product Hunt, beta launches, feature announcements, waitlists.
- `gtm-saas` — Go-to-market motions for SaaS: PLG, SLG, hybrid.
- `abm-saas` — Account-based marketing for SaaS selling to teams.
- `referral-program` — Referral, affiliate, ambassador program design.
- `free-tool-strategy` — Engineering-as-marketing: free tools for lead gen and SEO.
- `churn-prevention` — Cancel flows, save offers, dunning, win-back.

## Paid & Analytics

Spending money on acquisition and measuring the rest.

- `paid-ads` — Google, Meta, LinkedIn, X campaign strategy and optimization.
- `analytics-tracking` — GA4, GTM, event tracking, conversion setup.

## Pricing & RevOps

Monetization and the plumbing behind it.

- `pricing-strategy` — Tiers, packaging, freemium vs trial, price increases.
- `revops` — Lead scoring, routing, CRM hygiene, marketing-to-sales handoff.
- `sales-enablement` — Decks, one-pagers, objection handling, demo scripts.
- `competitor-alternatives` — Competitor comparison and alternative pages.

## Frameworks

Mental models that plug into other skills.

- `marketing-psychology` — 70+ cognitive biases and persuasion principles applied to marketing.
